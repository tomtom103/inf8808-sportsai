Our three finalists

After analyzing multiple aspects of the game, it is clear that three players stand out in terms of numbers and accolades. Our choice for the players who are the most likely to be on the podium is as follow :

Kylian Mbappe  Karim Benzema  Mohamed Salah

To decide between them, we will look at their contribution and influence on both sides of the field. Their defensive contribution as well as the danger that they bring on the offensive side will be analyzed.
