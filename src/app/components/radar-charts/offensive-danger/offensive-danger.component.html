Danger creation in the offensive phase

Scoring and shooting is good. But dribbling and sneaking into the opponent’s defense is also a very important skill. In this section, we will see which player is the best at creating an oppressive atmosphere in the opposing area.

Statistics Used:

CPA

Number of balls brought into the opponent's box

Succ

Number of successful ball presses

Fld

Number of faults caused

The most dangerous player is...

Mbappé comes first with an outstanding danger creation this season. With fifty-eight faults caused, Mbappé was able to contribute heavily into the offensive dynamic of his team.
