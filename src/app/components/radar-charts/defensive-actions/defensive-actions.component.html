Defensive contribution

As quoted by Sun Tzu : “Attack is the secret of defense; defense is the planning of an attack.” A good player must be versatile on the pitch. If everyone is defending from the opponent, even the attackers must know how to intercept the ball and initiate a counter-offensive action.

Let's now look at the defensive contribution of each of our players.

TklW Tackles that lead to regaining possession
Succ Number of successful ball presses
Int Number of successful interceptions
Blocks Everytime the player blocked a shot

This radar chart will help consider every variable and draw a general portrait of each player's defensive contribution.

This time, we can see that Mohamed Salah as a high defensive activity. His presence on the pitch allowed his team to regain possession of the ball multiple times this year. He leads in shot blocked, successfull presses & interceptions. He runs everywhere on the offensive portion of the field !

M'bappé also stands out with his number of successful tackles and almost equal amount of interceptions as Salah. Benzema on the other hand isn't as prolific in this area of the game stat-wise.
