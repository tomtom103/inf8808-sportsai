Defensive contribution

As quoted by Sun Tzu : “Attack is the secret of defense; defense is the planning of an attack.” A good player must be versatile on the pitch. If everyone is defending from the opponent, even the attackers must know how to intercept the ball and initiate a counter-offensive action.

Let's now look at the defensive contribution of each of our players.

Statistics Used:

TklW

Tackles that lead to regaining possession

Succ

Number of successful ball presses

Int

Number of successful interceptions

Blocks

Everytime the player blocked a shot

The results :

This radar chart will help consider every variable and draw a general portrait of each player's defensive skills.
