General statitics

Let’s start-off with the essential question : who’s the best player when it comes to scoring and passing ? This series of graphs will give a general understanding of the global efficiency and accuracy of each player for different metrics.

G - Goals Number of goals scored by the player
xG - Expected Goals Number of goals expected to be scored by the player
A - Assists Number of assist passes the player has given
xA - Expected Assists Number of assist passes expected to be given by the player
PK - Penalty Kicks Penalty kicks scored by the player
PKatt - Attempted penalty kicks Total number of penalty kicks, scored or not
GminPK - Goals minus penalty kicks Total number of goals without the penalty kicks
npxG - Non penalty expected goals The amount of expected goals from the player that are not penalties
Here's what it looks like :

Benzema and M’bappé stand out from the rest in every category.

We can see that every player exceeds what was expected for them , except for Sadio Mané. We can also see that Salah is barely over the expected numbers and he's really reliant on the goals scored by penalties.

Benzema is the exact opposite, as he missed a bunch of penalties but still exceeded expectation in terms of goals scored.

M’bappé seems to be the most productive this year, with his incredible goals and assists rates.
