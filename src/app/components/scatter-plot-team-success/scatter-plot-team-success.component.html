Impact on the team’s success

What’s a captain without his crew ? The collective aspect of the game is often underlooked. But a player who’s able to have a positive impact on his teammates is an invaluable quality. In this section, we look at the team dynamic when each player is on the field.

PPM - Point per matches Average points won by a team when the player was on the pitch.
onG Total goals scored by the team while the player is on the pitch
onGa Total goals conceded by the team while the player is on the pitch
+/- onG - onGa : whether the team scored or conceded more when the player was on the pitch
As far as impact goes:

The more the player is at the top right, the more his team manages to seek points while having a good +/- differential.

This is why a tooltip shows if the +/- is due to few goals conceded or many scored when the player is on the pitch.

As we can see, M’bappé stands out once again. When he was playing, the team scored a whopping eighty-six goals last season.

It's also pretty interesting to see that with an identical onG value, Benzema has the highest PPM stat (2.44) while Nkunku has the lowest (1.71).
